Person of the Year, 1927 - Present

Person of the Year is an annual issue of the United States news magazine Time. It profiles a person, a group, an idea, or an object that "for better or for worse" has done the most to influence the events of the year.

Use the charts below to see who has won from the perspective of the honor, the country, the category, and the year. Check out the table below for all details too! The Info page gives a little more background on Time.
