About the Magazine!
Time is a weekly news magazine published in the United States. It was founded in 1923. Time has the world's largest circulation for a weekly news magazine. The print edition has a readership of 26 million, 20 million of whom are based in the United States. In mid-2012, its circulation was over 3 million,which fell to 2 million by late 2017. Formerly published by Time Inc., Time is now published by TIME USA, LLC, owned by Marc Benioff since November 2018.
